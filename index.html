The Name

Her Heart.
Her Journey.
Her Wholeness.

HerQalb (قلب — Arabic for "heart") is a faith-based self-development brand for Muslim women in their 20s who are navigating love, identity, and purpose.

We exist at the intersection of Islamic principles and emotional healing — a space that has been largely underserved. Not for half-love or half-answers.

Our voice is the voice of a wise older friend. Someone who has been through it, who doesn't sugarcoat, but who holds your hand through every hard truth. Warm but not soft. Honest but never harsh.

What we stand for

Real guidance.
No filters. Rooted in faith.

Our Mission

To help Muslim women love themselves the way Allah already does

We create ebooks, content, and community experiences that reconnect women to their inherent worth — through Islamic principles, honest conversation, and practical tools for growth.

Our Vision

A generation of Muslim women who never settle

HerQalb is building a community where faith and self-worth are inseparable. Not for half-love, half-answers, or a version of themselves that feels like less than enough.

Our Audience

Muslim women in their 20s feeling lost or stuck

She's spiritually curious, emotionally intelligent, and tired of content that feels performative. She values authenticity above all else and can detect anything that isn't real from a mile away.

Our Promise

Real guidance. No filters. Rooted in faith.

We ask hard questions, sit with difficult truths, and offer a path forward that is grounded in the deen and honest about the journey.
